Iconic natural wonder.

The Grand Canyon, located in Arizonais a massive natural wonder carved by the Colorado River over millions of years. It boasts stunning rock formations and breathtaking vistas, drawing visitors from around the world to marvel at its beauty and geological significance.

Sedona

Red rock formations and spiritual retreats.

Distance from nearest city: 120 miles

Modes of transportation available: Car, bus, hiking tours

Location: Latitude 34°52′07″N, Longitude 111°44′03″W (North of Phoenix, Arizona)

Sedona, nestled in the heart of Arizona, is renowned for its majestic red rock formations and spiritual allure. Visitors are captivated by the stunning scenery and vibrant energy of this desert town, which offers opportunities for outdoor adventure, spiritual rejuvenation, and artistic inspiration.

Antelope Canyon

Slot canyon with stunning rock formations.

Distance from nearest city: 130 miles

Modes of transportation available: Car, guided tours

Location: Latitude 36°51′51.48″N, Longitude 111°24′32.04″W (Near Page, Arizona)

Antelope Canyon, located near Page, Arizona, is a mesmerizing slot canyon renowned for its captivating light beams and swirling sandstone formations. Carved over millions of years by wind and water, this natural wonder offers a surreal and otherworldly experience for photographers, adventurers, and nature enthusiasts alike.

Lowell Observatory

Astronomical observatory known for its discoveries.

Distance from nearest city: 2 miles

Modes of transportation available: Car, shuttle service

Location: Latitude 35°12′07″N, Longitude 111°39′00″W (Near Flagstaff, Arizona)

The Lowell Observatory, situated in Flagstaff, Arizona, is a historic astronomical research facility known for its groundbreaking discoveries and contributions to the field of astronomy. The discovery of Pluto was made by astronomer Clyde Tombaugh while he was working at Lowell Observatory. Founded in 1894, it continues to inspire curiosity and scientific exploration, offering visitors the chance to gaze at distant galaxies and ponder the mysteries of the cosmos.
